Game Guide

Welcome to the Pasugame! This is an endless runner game where you control a dino to avoid obstacles and collect boxes that give you coins.

Use the jump and slide buttons (or keyboard keys W/S or arrows) to avoid obstacles. Collect as many boxes as possible to earn coins.

Spend your coins in the Character menu to buy new characters. Your progress and coins are saved automatically.
